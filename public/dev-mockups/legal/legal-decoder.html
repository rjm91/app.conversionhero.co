1. TERM. The term shall commence on March 1, 2026 and continue for twelve (12) months, terminating February 28, 2027, and shall automatically renew on a month-to-month basis absent written notice not less than sixty (60) days prior.

2. RENT. Lessee shall pay $1,450 per month, due on the first day of each month. A late charge of $75 shall accrue if rent remains unpaid after the fifth (5th) day. Lessee shall remit a security deposit of $1,450.

3. DEFAULT. In the event Lessee fails to cure any default within three (3) days of written notice, Lessor may pursue all remedies, including eviction and acceleration of all sums due.

The gist

You're renting an apartment for $1,450/mo for 12 months. It auto-renews unless you give 60 days' notice. Miss rent and you have just 3 days to fix it before eviction can start. Nothing predatory, but the auto-renew and short cure period are worth noting.
